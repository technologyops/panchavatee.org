Calendar

Calendar gives the periodical festivals at the temple. The following are the yearly Calendar of events:
  • Sri Rama Navami - March & April 2004.

  • Milk Abhishekam in Rahu Kala (4.30pm - 6.00pm) - First Sunday of every month from January 2004 onwards.

  • Music Festival after Hanumath Jayanthi.

  • New Year eve - Midnight, Heralding the new year with Sahasranama Swarna Pushpa Archana.

  • Installation day - 11 June 2004 Commomerating installation of deity.
