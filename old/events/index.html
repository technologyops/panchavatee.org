Poojas and Sevas to Panchamukha Sri Anjaneya Swamy

Poojas and Sevas are carried out to Panchamukha Sri Anjaneya Swamy as per the Sampradhaya. Everyday the temple opens early in the morning with the Kalasannidhi pooja and all other Archanas, Neivaidyam and Prasadams are offered 6 times a day. On special occassions, special sevas and poojas are performed in effort to multiply the spiritual power of the Temple.
