Jayamangala Valampuri Sri Mahaganapati

Lord Sri Ganesh or Vinayaka, is the remover of all hurdles and obstacles. Lord Jayamangala Valampuri Sri Mahaganapati has his trunk curled to the right, inspiring His devotees to be always righteous and ethical in their thought, word and deed and thus achieve lasting success.
