Calendar

Calendar gives the periodical festivals at the temple. The following are Calendar of events:
  • Sri Rama Navami - March & April.
  • Tamil New Year's day - in April, every year.
  • Sankatahara chathurthi for Mahaganapathy - every month.
  • Poonarvasu star celebrations for Sri Rama - every month.
  • Moola natchatra celebrations for Anjaneyaswamy - every month.
  • Sita Rama Kalyana Utsavam - daily.
  • Navarathri celebrations - during dasara.
  • Hanumath Jeyanthi celebrations with Music Concerts - annual.
  • Vinayaka Chaturthi celebrations - every year.
