Regular Sevas to Panchamukha Sri Anjaneya Swamy

Regular Poojas are carried out every day from the early morning till night at the Temple. These Sevas are performed by the temple as well as by the devotees. The Sevas that are performed to Sri Anjaneya Swamy every day are:

Time Name of Seva Done by
7.00am - 7.30am Sandyakala Pooja, Kalasandhi Pooja and Neivaidyam Temple/Devotee
7.30am - 12.00pm Regular Ashtothram Devotee
12.00pm - 12.15pm Uchhikala Pooja and Neivaidyam Temple/Devotee
4.00pm - 7.00pm Sayaraksha Pooja Temple/Devotee
7.00pm - 8.00pm Sahasranama Archana Temple/Devotee
8.45pm - 9.00pm Ardhajama Pooja Milk and dry fruits Neivaidyam Temple/Devotee
