Location: Panchavatee is located on the 29th km from Tindivanam towards Pondicherry. It is 9km from Pondicherry towards Tindivanam. It is 150km and two and a half hour drive by car from Chennai.

Travelling to Panchavatee: It can be reached by all buses leaving Chennai for Pondicherry, Cuddalore, Chidambaram.
 
Accomodation: Nearest point to stay is Pondicherry where quite a few hotels are available of all grades including the Pondicherry Ashram where good rooms are available at low tariff.
