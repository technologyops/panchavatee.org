After the installation of the Viswaroopa Panchamukha Anjeneya Swamy Vigraham on 12th June 2003, his divine command took three and half years to complete the magnificent Temple for him.The ardhamandapam, and the sprawling maha-mandapam providea spectacular view of Sri Panchmukha Anjeneya Swamy from the very entrance itself. The sannidhis for Jayamangala Valampuri Maha Ganapathy and Jayamangala Pattabhisheka Sri Ramachandra Prabhu are striking for their richness of traditional south-indian sculpture, art, and design.

The kumbabhishekam for this Temple was performed in a grand scale between the 27th and 31st of January 2007. It was an unparalleld function for the devotion, gaiety, and religious fervour witnessed, on all these 5 days, by several thousands of devotees. Five days of various homams, as prescribed in the agama sastras, culminated with the consecration of the kalasams on the most auspicious kumbhabishekam day on 31st January 2007. The sanctified holy waters were poured over the divine kalasams and His command for a unique Temple for him, thus fulfilled witnessed by nearly two lakh devotees.

Annadhanam was performed on all 5 days of kumbhabishekam celebrations and as a fitting finale, on the last day nearly two lakh devotees were treated to a sumptuous meal as his prasadam after His darshan.
