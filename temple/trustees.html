Panchamukha Sri Jayamaruthi Seva Trust and Panchamukha Sri Jayamruthi Charitable Trust

Two Trusts have been formed. The Panchamukha Sri Jayamaruthi Seva Trust is entrusted with all Capital Works for the Temple. The Panchamukha Sri Jayamangala Charitable Trust will take care of all the charitable activities associated with this Kshetram.

The Trustees besides being prominent in their respective fields, are dedicated devotees of Lord Anjaneya. In their devotion they consider themselves as the instruments of the Lord for implementing the various divine directives emerging out of the Deva Prashnams. The construction of this Magnificient Temple for Jayamangala Panchamukha Sri Anjaneyaswamy and related multifaceted infrastructure facilities are only the first stage of implementation of this Divine Wish. By the Grace of the Lord, and in compliance with the revelations of the Deva Prashnams, the Trusts have ambitious plans to create facilities for Annadhanam, Veda Patashala, Free Medical Centre for the poor and several other charitable activities for the benefit of the underprivileged, the physically challenged and the spiritually needy segments of society. The aims and objectives of the Trusts also incorporate the setting up of a world quality Centre of Excellence for Advanced Education and Research with appropriate associated libraries with multimedia facilities. By the Grace of the Lord, it is the sincere and profound prayer of the Trustees that with the active and devoted support of all their fellow devotees, all these plans will bear fruit in suitable phases not long from now.

The first priority is the for the Temple Construction and the stages in which this is being envisioned are set out.

On return from a trip to Tirumala, Sri Anna prayed to Lord Anjaneya to guide him suitably in the project and present him with pious and committed devotees as trustees for the project.
